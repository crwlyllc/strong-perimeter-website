Common repairs

We repair the weak points that make a fence look tired or stop working.

Repair work can be small and targeted or part of a larger restoration plan. The first step is understanding what failed and whether the rest of the line is stable.

What this usually includes

  • Wrought iron rust, broken sections, bent pickets, and loose panels.
  • Wood fence pickets, rails, posts, gates, leaning sections, and storm damage.
  • Chain link fabric, rails, posts, tension, and access issues.
  • Pipe fence rust, bent rails, broken sections, and loose posts.

Project detail

Vinyl cracked panels, damaged posts, and alignment issues.

Questions

Common questions before requesting a quote.

Can you repair one section of fence?

Yes. If the rest of the fence is in usable shape, a targeted repair may be the right scope.

Do you repair commercial fences?

Yes. Strong Perimeter handles residential and commercial fence repair.

When is replacement better than repair?

Replacement is usually better when posts, structure, or too many sections are failing at once.
