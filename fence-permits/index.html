Questions

Common questions

Do all DFW fence projects require permits?

No. Requirements vary by city, property, height, location, pool use, and project scope. Confirm current rules before work starts.

Can Strong Perimeter check permit questions with me?

Yes. We can help identify what should be confirmed before a fence quote moves forward.

Are HOA approvals separate from city permits?

Often, yes. A project may need HOA approval even when the city permit path is simple.
